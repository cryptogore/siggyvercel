OVERVIEW

Ritual is an ecosystem designed to enable developers and communities to build and interact with AI systems.

The goal of Ritual is to create an open environment where artificial intelligence systems, models, and tools can be developed collaboratively. Ritual connects developers, infrastructure, and intelligent applications into a unified ecosystem.

The platform encourages innovation, experimentation, and collaboration between developers who are interested in building AI-powered systems.

Ritual focuses on enabling intelligent systems that interact with one another through shared infrastructure.

--------------------------------------------------

VISION OF RITUAL

The vision of Ritual is to expand access to artificial intelligence development and empower builders to create intelligent systems within an open ecosystem.

Rather than isolated tools or standalone applications, Ritual aims to build a collaborative network where AI models, infrastructure, and developer tools work together.

By creating an ecosystem for AI innovation, Ritual enables developers to explore new ways of building intelligent applications.

--------------------------------------------------

RITUAL ECOSYSTEM

The Ritual ecosystem is composed of several key elements:

Developers  
Developers are responsible for building tools, applications, and AI systems within the ecosystem.

AI Models  
AI models are systems that perform tasks such as text generation, analysis, or predictions.

Infrastructure  
Infrastructure allows AI models and applications to run and interact with one another.

Applications  
Applications built within the ecosystem allow users to interact with AI systems.

Community  
The Ritual community consists of developers and users who collaborate and share knowledge.
